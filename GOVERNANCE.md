# Governance

ORBI uses a BDFL model during the `0.x` series, with Tej / Adversyn Labs as the initial maintainer.
Public API, protocol, persistence, licensing, and security-model changes require an RFC under
`docs/rfcs/` before implementation.

A core-maintainer committee may replace the BDFL model after a stable `1.0` release and sustained
community participation. Contributions use Developer Certificate of Origin sign-off (`git commit
-s`) rather than a separate contributor licence agreement.
